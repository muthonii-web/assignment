About My Journey

In the heart of indian ocean lies a jewel called Zanzibar, where i spent an unforgettable week exploring its pristine beaches,historical stone town and vibrate culture.It was a transformative experience that changed my perspective on life.

from the moment i stepped onto the white sands beaches to my wandering through the narrow alleys of stone town, everyday brought new adventures.The islands rich history as a trading hub, its spices plantation and the warmth of its people created an enchanting of experience
